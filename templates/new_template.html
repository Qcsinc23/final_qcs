About Templates

Event templates help you quickly create common event types with their associated equipment needs. They save time and ensure consistency.

Template Uses
  • Create events faster by pre-selecting equipment
  • Ensure consistent setup for repeating events
  • Standardize events by type or client needs
  • Simplify staff training with standard configurations
